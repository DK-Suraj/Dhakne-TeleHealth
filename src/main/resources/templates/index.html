Portal Usage Guidelines

First, check your email accounts' Spam/Promotions folder. If it is missing there, ensure your mail address pattern matches your actual sign-up credentials or trigger a recovery check after 5 minutes.

Simply report to the corresponding department desk 10 minutes before your allocated slot time and present your digital dashboard token number to bypass the physical registration desks instantly.

For data protection and privacy control, user roles are strictly validated via security filters. If your session expires or you try to view logs without an active account token, our system automatically routes you back to secure sign-in paths.
